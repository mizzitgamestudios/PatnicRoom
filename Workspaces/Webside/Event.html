Overview

Open Quest-design

To support the dynamic Enviroment of the gameloop,
we decided for a broad definition of goals

At Generation,the name-giving Panicroom is placed one of the 100 by 100 sized Map. After finding it,the only prerequisit to enter it is standing on the Tile.

The way on how its done is completly up to the player.

reactionary Sandboxes

Every Tile on P@tnicRooms map has its own set of attributes which determinds their Behaviour.
Grass can be set on Fire and get extinguished as well.

Each of those attributes are universally applyable,this garantees a dynamic and breathing world.

fluid Classes

Levelprogression is only tied to the Panicroom,entering it grands you a new slot for Kits.

Kits are broken up in thre parts: Base-function, specialication and overaching benefits

There are nine types of Kits on three perceptable Layers
