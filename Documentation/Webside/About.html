About the game

where man meets magic and machine

We are great fans of the Cyberpunk genre and especially of the Shadowrun pen and paper.
Its lore and our fleshed out worldbuilding will be the basis of Setting.


Enter a world which combines wise wizzards with terrifying terminators, where the definition of man and nature gets blurry.

Its wide casting net of themes can create chaotic scenes with many possibilities to interact.
we are eager to develop a system which lets you act out in creative improvised ways, spiraling out in even more absurdity.

Your Goal: find the P@nicroom!

our crafted world can be dangerous and harsh,to survive you need to up your arsenal.
to progress you you crafting of tools you need to enter a Shelter,the p@nicroom.
but there is a catch,every shelter progresses your just crafting once,you have to venture to keep pace


every Chunk has the size of 100 x 100 tiles,and one is your Goal.
The Green circle symbolices your last save place of the area

their procedural generated by handcrafted parts and merged together,also every chunk comes with keywords which descripe a special enviromental challenge



Live. Get crazy. Die. Repeat.

Our intented playtime of walkthorugh is about 4 hours maximum,
this is to emphasize its experimental nature.


Unfamiliar situations can be deadly in particular,but death is your best teacher.
players are able to get an awareness of the underlying gamesystem and can abuse them to their advatage.

for example,beginning a new game lets the player choose one random kit by a kategory,
or they can type out their name,players who stay aware and are perceptive are strongly rewarded
